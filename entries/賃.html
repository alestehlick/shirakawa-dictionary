賃
Uncategorized
Kun: yatou, kariru  |  On: chin, jin
wages ・ rent ・ to hire ・ to borrow

Explanation

A phono-semantic character with 任 as the phonetic element signaling the Sino-Japanese reading; in the Shuowen it is glossed as “to employ.” It denotes engaging people and paying them money as compensation for their work—hence words for wages. Beyond hiring, its use extends to borrowing or renting for a fee, as seen in compounds like chintaku, “a rented house.”
