発
Uncategorized
Kun: tatsu  |  On: hatsu, hotsu, ho
to start ・ to set in motion ・ to depart ・ to emit ・ to occur ・ to reveal ・ to express ・ to discover ・ to invent

Explanation

The original form is 發, combining 癶, which shows both feet poised to move—an attitude of ready departure—with a bow and 殳, the hand wielding a striking implement. Together they depict the first arrow loosed to announce the opening of hostilities. From this inaugural shot comes the core idea of initiating movement or action: to start, to depart, to send forth or emit, and, by extension, for something latent to come into the open—events breaking out, matters being discovered or invented, and feelings or secrets being revealed or expressed.
