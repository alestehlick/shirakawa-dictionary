故
Uncategorized
Kun: yue, kotosara, moto  |  On: ko
reason ・ cause ・ therefore ・ intentional ・ accident ・ old ・ former

Explanation

A compound character that joins 古 and 攴. In Shirakawa’s view, 古 shows a sacred shield (kan/tate) set upon a covenant vessel (sai) that held written prayers, expressing the long preservation of their efficacy. With the addition of 攴, the hand that strikes, the graph comes to depict an act that strikes against and thus intentionally undermines that preserved efficacy. From this arise senses like “deliberately” (kotosara) and the notion of an adverse occurrence seen in 事故. Through association with yue “reason,” 故 also denotes the grounds or justification for something, while continuing to carry older nuances such as “old” and “former.”
