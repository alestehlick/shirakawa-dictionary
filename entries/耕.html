耕
Uncategorized
Kun: tagayasu  |  On: kou
to plow ・ to till ・ to cultivate

Explanation

A phono-semantic character: the left element 耒 depicts the plow, anchoring the graph in the world of agriculture, while the right element 井 serves as the phonetic, marking the on-reading in the same way it does in related characters. Traditional lexica also record the variant form 畊. With the plow as its core image, the character expresses the act of working the soil—ploughing and cultivating fields.
