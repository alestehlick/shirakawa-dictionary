操
Uncategorized
Kun: ayatsuru, misao  |  On: sou
to manipulate ・ to operate ・ chastity ・ moral integrity

Explanation

操 is a phono-semantic compound: the hand on the left marks an action of grasping or handling, while the phonetic element 喿 on the right supplies the sound sō and the core image. In ancient forms, 喿 shows a tree hung with many mouth-shaped votive receptacles into which petitions to the gods were placed; people gathered there to pray aloud. This phonetic appears in 噪 and 譟 for noisy praying, and in 燥 for the anxious fervor of prayer. With the hand added, 操 depicts holding fast to that prayer-laden tree, controlling and steadying it while praying wholeheartedly for a wish to be fulfilled. From this comes the sense of taking in hand, handling, and skillfully manipulating, and by extension the moral sense of misao—steadfast principle and unchanging fidelity of will.
