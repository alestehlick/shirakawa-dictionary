花
Uncategorized
Kun: hana  |  On: ka
flower ・ blossom ・ beauty ・ gorgeousness

Explanation

Flower is a later picto‑phonetic character: 化 serves as the phonetic that signals the On reading ka. Its model is the older graph 華, a pictograph of a splendid blossom fully open. The simplified form 花 appears from about the fifth century, and it names the blossoms of grasses and trees; by extension it evokes beauty and the bright, showy splendor likened to flowers.
