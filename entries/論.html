論
Uncategorized
Kun:  |  On: ron
argument ・ discussion ・ to discuss ・ to debate ・ to explain

Explanation

論 is a phono-semantic character formed with the speech element 言 and the phonetic 侖. The phonetic 侖, which also appears in 崙 and 掄, originally means things laid or piled in orderly sequence, and here it supplies both sound and the notion of arrangement. The character thus evokes words set out in order. In the Shuowen it is glossed as ‘to consult, deliberate,’ the process of stating one’s views and contending toward agreement. From this, 論 comes to mean to argue and discuss, to inquire into a matter, and to set forth or explain.
