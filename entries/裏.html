裏
Uncategorized
Kun: ura, uchi  |  On: ri
reverse side ・ lining ・ back ・ inside

Explanation

A phono-semantic character in which 里 serves as the phonetic, pointing to the on-yomi ri. In the Shuowen it is explained as the inside of a garment, that is, the reverse or lining, and from this core sense it came to mean the back, reverse, and interior more generally. The Book of Odes illustrates this with the line “green robe, yellow lining” (綠衣黃裏), clearly using 裏 for the lining. Bronze inscriptions likewise show 裏 in contexts such as a ceremonial gift of red cloth backed with tiger hide, an emblem hung on a chariot—usage that highlights the notion of a backing or inner side. 裡 is a later popular variant form.
