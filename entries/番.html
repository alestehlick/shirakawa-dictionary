番
Uncategorized
Kun:  |  On: ban, han
number ・ order ・ turn ・ watch ・ duty

Explanation

This character began as a pictograph of the back of an animal’s paw, with the upper element 釆 portraying a claw, so that the whole figure shows the paw’s top side complete with claws. The ancient Shuowen already glossed it as “an animal’s paw.” In later writing the anatomical sense was assigned to 蹯, while 番 developed into meanings of alternation and orderly succession—one’s turn, a numbered sequence, and standing watch or duty taken in rotation.
