Uncategorized
Kun: masu  |  On: eki, yaku
benefit ・ profit ・ increase

Explanation

In the bronze and seal scripts, the graph is a pictograph: two forked ends of a cord drawn together and tied in a knot. This is the original form of the character read i, meaning to tie or constrict (kubiru), vividly capturing the act of binding and narrowing.
